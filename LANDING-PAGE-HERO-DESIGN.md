## ğŸ¨ Key Design Decisions

### 1. Headline Structure
- **Main headline**: "GDPR Compliance Testing"
- **Accent line**: "for Privacy Professionals" (blue)
- **Why**: Immediately identifies tool category and target audience

### 2. Subheadline
- **What it does**: "Automatically detect consent violations..."
- **Who it's for**: "Built for DPOs, developers, and compliance teams"
- **Why**: Clear value prop + audience identification in one sentence

### 3. Quick Benefits
- **Visual icons**: Checkmark, lightning, lock
- **Short labels**: Easy to scan
- **Why**: Builds trust without overwhelming

### 4. Scanner Card
- **Elevated design**: White card with shadow
- **Prominent placement**: Below hero, above fold
- **Why**: Makes the CTA (scan action) the focal point
